  • Area: Audio
  • Position: Sound Design Lead
  • Game: League of Legends: Wild Rift
Description:

As Sound Design Lead, you will own the sonic experience across all of Wild Rift, globally. You will guide and oversee external teams to develop Wild Rift's own unique and iconic sound. Through your close partnerships with Riot and Tencent stakeholders and developers, you will ensure that Wild Rift delivers a cohesive and resonant experience for players. You will report into the Creative Director.

Responsabilities:

Uphold the sonic expression of the League of Legends IP, Supervise each segment of the audio pipeline for sound design, music and voice over, Partner with Riot LAX partners and Tencent developers on thematically cohesive sounds, music and voice over for cross-game tentpole moments, Act as a liaison to advocate for audio to the engineering staff, and partner with team members across disciplines to find solutions that best support quality audio.

Required qualifications:
  • 5+ years experience developing audio for games
  • 3+ years experience working in a leadership capacity
  • Expertise in the holistic game audio process
  • Exceptional Wwise or Fmod implementation skills, Knowledge of sound design creation tools (i.e. - ProTools, Nuendo, Reaper, Izotope, Waves, Native Instruments) and techniques
  • Knowledge of audio middleware (i.e. - Wwise, Fmod, Miles) and overall game engine technology (i.e. - Unreal, Unity, Lumberyard)
  • perience working with external developers
  • Fluency in English
Our perks:
  • Full relocation support
  • Full health insurance for you, your spouse, and children
  • Open paid time off
  • Retirement benefits with company matching
  • Life insurance, parental leave, plus short-term and long-term disability
  • Play Fund so you can broaden and deepen your knowledge of our players and community through games
  • We will double down on your donations of time and money to non-profits
